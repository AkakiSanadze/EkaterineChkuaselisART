About

The Artist

An artist from Tbilisi, Georgia.

Education

1992-1998 – Graduated from Tbilisi State Academy of Arts, Faculty of Monumental Painting

Exhibitions

2003 – National Gallery, Participates in Group Exhibitions, Tbilisi
2005 – Gallery "Karvasla", Participates in Group Exhibitions, Tbilisi
2005 – Gallery "Kopala", Solo Exhibition, Tbilisi
2008 – Sheraton Metechi Palace Exhibition, Tbilisi
2012 – Tiflis Gallery, Solo Exhibition, Tbilisi

Works are also part of private collections in various countries (Tbilisi, London, Strasbourg, Moscow, Berlin).
